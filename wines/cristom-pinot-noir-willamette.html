üåç The Willamette Valley Region

Oregon's Willamette Valley is located between the Coast Range and Cascade Mountains, creating a cool-climate paradise for Pinot Noir. The region's volcanic and marine sedimentary soils, combined with a long, cool growing season, produce wines of remarkable elegance and complexity. The Eola-Amity Hills AVA, where Cristom is located, is known for its cool Pacific winds and volcanic Jory soils, producing wines with bright acidity and distinctive minerality.
