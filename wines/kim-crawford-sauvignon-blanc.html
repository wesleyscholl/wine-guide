üåç The Marlborough Region

Marlborough, located at the northern tip of New Zealand's South Island, is the country's largest and most famous wine region. The sunny days and cool nights create perfect conditions for Sauvignon Blanc, resulting in wines with intense aromatics and vibrant acidity. The region's stony, well-drained soils contribute to the distinctive mineral character found in these wines.
