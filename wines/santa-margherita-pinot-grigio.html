üåç The Alto Adige Region

Alto Adige (also known as South Tyrol) is Italy's northernmost wine region, nestled in the Dolomite Alps near the Austrian border. The high altitude, cool nights, and Alpine breezes create ideal conditions for white wines with vibrant acidity and pure fruit expression. The region's Germanic influences are evident in both the culture and the wine styles.
