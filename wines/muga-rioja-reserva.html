üåç The Rioja Region

Rioja, located in northern Spain along the Ebro River, is Spain's most prestigious wine region. Protected by the Sierra de Cantabria mountains, the region enjoys a unique microclimate influenced by both Atlantic and Mediterranean weather patterns. Rioja is divided into three zones: Rioja Alta, Rioja Alavesa, and Rioja Oriental, each contributing distinct characteristics. The Reserva classification requires minimum aging of 3 years, with at least 1 year in oak.
