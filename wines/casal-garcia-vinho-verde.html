üåç The Vinho Verde Region

Vinho Verde, meaning "green wine," is Portugal's most northerly wine region, characterized by its lush, green landscapes and Atlantic-influenced climate. The region's granite soils and cool temperatures produce wines with bright acidity and natural effervescence. Despite the name, Vinho Verde wines are meant to be consumed young and fresh.
