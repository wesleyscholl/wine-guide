üåç The Sonoma County Region

Sonoma County is Napa's neighbor and rival, offering diverse terroirs from coastal Chardonnay to inland Cabernet. The region's cooler nights and varied elevations produce wines with both power and elegance. Decoy sources fruit from premium Sonoma vineyards, creating wines that express the best of California winemaking without the Napa Valley price premium.
