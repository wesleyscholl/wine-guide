üåç Where It Comes From

Abruzzo sits along Italy's Adriatic coast, protected by the Apennine Mountains. The region's unique microclimate - warm days and cool nights from mountain breezes - allows Montepulciano grapes to develop rich flavors while maintaining fresh acidity. The terrain is rugged and beautiful, producing wines of character and authenticity that reflect centuries of winemaking tradition.
