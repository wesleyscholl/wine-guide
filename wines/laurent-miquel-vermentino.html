üçá About This Wine

Laurent Miquel is a family-owned estate in the Languedoc region of Southern France, known for producing exceptional value wines with Mediterranean character. Their Vermentino showcases this Corsican grape variety (also known as Rolle in France and Pigato in Italy) at its finest. The Miquel family has been making wine in the region for generations, combining traditional techniques with modern winemaking to create wines that express the sunny, herb-scented terroir of the Mediterranean south.
