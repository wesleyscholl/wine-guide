üåç The Columbia Valley Region

Washington's Columbia Valley is one of America's premier wine regions, located in the rain shadow of the Cascade Mountains. The region enjoys long, warm summer days and cool nights, creating ideal conditions for Cabernet Sauvignon. The volcanic and wind-deposited loess soils, combined with minimal rainfall and abundant sunshine, produce wines with intense fruit concentration, firm structure, and excellent aging potential. Columbia Valley has firmly established itself among the world's great Cabernet regions.
