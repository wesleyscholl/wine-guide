üåç The Mosel Region

The Mosel is Germany's most prestigious Riesling region, known for its dramatic steep slate vineyards along the winding Mosel River. The blue and gray slate soils give Mosel Rieslings their distinctive minerality and elegance. The cool climate and long growing season allow grapes to develop intense flavors while maintaining high acidity ‚Äî the hallmark of world-class Riesling.
