üåç Where It Comes From

Minervois sits in the heart of the Languedoc-Roussillon region, nestled between the Montagne Noire and the Canal du Midi. The landscape is dotted with scrubby garrigue - the aromatic Mediterranean herbs (thyme, rosemary, lavender) that infuse the wines with their distinctive character. Hot, dry summers and cooling winds from the Mediterranean create ideal conditions for ripe, concentrated fruit.
